Biografia

Salvatore Caporale è un professionista dell’aviazione con oltre quarant’anni di esperienza tra ambito civile e militare. Con più di 7.000 ore di volo, di cui oltre 4.000 dedicate alla formazione, ha costruito una reputazione internazionale come Senior Flight Instructor & Examiner su diversi modelli AW (AW109, AW139, AW169, AW189/189K). Dopo una carriera come Ufficiale in reparti elicotteri per operazioni speciali, è passato al settore della formazione occupandosi successivamente anche del settore corporate e VIP, diventando uno dei principali esperti nelle Head of State operations. Attualmente guida le Middle East Operations Services per Leonardo Helicopters, supervisionando customer support, training e sviluppo strategico nell’area del Golfo.

Biography

Salvatore Caporale is an aviation professional with over fourty years of combined civil and military experience. With more than 7,000 flight hours, including over 4,000 dedicated to training, he has built an international reputation as a Senior Flight Instructor & Examiner on multiple AW models (AW109, AW139, AW169, AW189/189K). After a distinguished career as an Officer in Special Operations helicopter units, he transitioned into the training sector eventually dealing also with corporate and VIP aviation sector, becoming one of the leading experts in Head of State operations. He currently leads Middle East Operations Services for Leonardo Helicopters, overseeing customer support, training, and strategic development across the Gulf region.
